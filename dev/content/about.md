# About Spotlight Homeschool Community Theater

## Mission Statement

Spotlight Homeschool Community Theater of West Michigan is a **FREE homeschool theater** dedicated to providing wholesome, faith-based, and family-friendly entertainment for all ages and abilities.

## What We Do

Our mission is to foster the development of young performers by providing opportunities to:

- **Develop Public Speaking Skills** - Students work directly on stage in front of live audiences
- **Enhance Creative Abilities** - Through character development, stage presence, and artistic expression
- **Build Community** - Provide wholesome, faith-based entertainment that brings families together
- **Encourage Confidence** - Help young performers gain self-esteem through the performing arts

## Organization Type

**Performance Art Theatre**

## Social Impact

We believe that theater education and community performances strengthen bonds within families and our local faith community. All performances are designed to be family-friendly and accessible to audiences of all ages.
