## Media Usage Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- Use full-color logo on light backgrounds
- Maintain minimum size of 100px for web
- Ensure adequate white space around logo
- Do not distort or alter proportions

### Photo Usage
- Give appropriate credit: "Spotlight Homeschool Community Theater of West Michigan"
- Obtain permission before commercial use
- Link to official social media accounts
- Include attribution in image descriptions
